The recognition of isiThonga language is based on the ethos of our school. The history of the school is projected in the name Thongaland itself. The school was created in honour of the people of Thongaland both alive and dead. Thongaland refer to the geographic & historical location while VaRhonga (baThonga) refer to the people.

Thanks To Get Started With Our School

Early Education

Your child becoming their happiest and most confident self. Our science-informed learning framework, Discovery Driven Learning®, focuses on your child’s individual strengths. And our highly trained teachers meet your child where they are, nurture their full potential, and partner with you in their development and future success.

Preschool Program

Preschool is a big year. We’ve designed our program to give you complete confidence in every aspect of your child’s experience; from health and safety; to ready-for-school curriculum; to the full support of experienced teachers. Just as important, our program is based on what research says best prepares children for kindergarten—and all the big steps ahead.

Read More
Toddler Care

When your toddler’s in the right place, you just know. Our program is designed to give you that feeling — a safe place for your newly independent child to test out and learn new skills, wonder about everything, and discover how to make friends. It’s all alongside teachers who know every day that they’re not caring for any child, but the most important child — yours.

Read More
Kindergarten Prep

Kindergarten is a time of enormous and exciting growth. As a parent, you want your child to enjoy this stage while building the right skills for the steps ahead. We offer a program that gives you both; teachers who cherish this important time, and curriculum recognized by parents and teachers for helping children build the social and academic skills needed to be ready for school.

Our Curriculum

  1. Project & Play Based: Immersive learning experiences that foster a joyful childhood, encourage relationship-building, and develop a foundation for future success.
  2. Whole Child Curriculum: Support for all skills — cognitive, linguistic, social-emotional, physical — to develop future learners, leaders, and citizens.
  3. Individualized Learning for Each Child: A one-of-a-kind child assessment process that provides teachers with real-time insight into children’s development that can inform lesson planning and teaching.
